# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025
**Last Updated:** January 1, 2025

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Sprunki Rejoyed ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information related to your use of our free browser-based music creation game.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information
When you visit and play Sprunki Rejoyed, we may automatically collect:

- **Browser Information:** Browser type, version, and language preferences
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, screen resolution
- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, time spent playing, gameplay interactions
- **Technical Data:** IP address, referring URLs, access times

### Information You Provide
We do not require account creation or personal information to play Sprunki Rejoyed. Any information you voluntarily provide (such as through contact forms or support requests) is handled according to this policy.

---

## 2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

- **Provide the Game:** Deliver and maintain the Sprunki Rejoyed gaming experience
- **Improve Performance:** Optimize game performance and fix technical issues
- **Analytics:** Understand how players interact with the game to improve features
- **Security:** Detect and prevent technical issues, fraud, or abuse
- **Legal Compliance:** Comply with applicable laws and regulations

---

## 3. Cookies and Similar Technologies

### What We Use
- **Essential Cookies:** Required for basic game functionality
- **Analytics Cookies:** Help us understand game usage and performance
- **Preference Cookies:** Remember your settings (like language choice)

### Cookie Control
You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect game functionality.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

### Advertising
We may display advertisements through third-party advertising networks (including Google AdSense). These networks may collect information about your visits to serve relevant ads. Please refer to their privacy policies:

- Google AdSense Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- Google Ad Settings: https://adssettings.google.com/

### Analytics
We may use analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to understand game usage. These services collect anonymous usage data.

### Content Delivery
We use content delivery networks (CDNs) to ensure fast game loading globally.

---

## 5. Data Retention

- **Gameplay Data:** Not stored permanently; session-based only
- **Analytics Data:** Aggregated and anonymized; retained for up to 2 years
- **Log Files:** Server logs retained for up to 90 days for security purposes
- **Contact Data:** Support inquiries retained for up to 1 year

---

## 6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect information:

- **HTTPS Encryption:** All communications encrypted via TLS
- **Secure Hosting:** Game hosted on secure cloud infrastructure
- **Access Controls:** Limited access to any collected data
- **Regular Monitoring:** Security monitoring for potential threats

While we strive to protect your information, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

Sprunki Rejoyed is designed to be family-friendly and suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

---

## 8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

- **Access:** Request information about data we hold about you
- **Correction:** Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your data
- **Opt-Out:** Opt out of certain data collection (via browser settings)
- **Data Portability:** Request a copy of your data in machine-readable format

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

---

## 9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

---

## 10. Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers support "Do Not Track" (DNT) signals. Currently, we do not respond to DNT signals, but you can control tracking through cookie settings and browser extensions.

---

## 11. California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (Note: We do not sell personal information)
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

---

## 12. European Union Users (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

- **Legal Basis:** We process data based on legitimate interests (game operation) and consent (cookies)
- **Data Protection Officer:** Contact information provided below
- **Right to Lodge Complaint:** You may file a complaint with your local data protection authority
