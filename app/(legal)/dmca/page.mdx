If you believe that content on Sprunki Rejoyed infringes your copyright, please send a DMCA takedown notice to our designated agent:

**DMCA Contact:**

- **Subject Line:** "DMCA Takedown Notice"

---

## 3. Required Information for DMCA Notice

Your DMCA notice must include the following information to be valid:

### 1. Identification of Copyrighted Work
A description of the copyrighted work you claim has been infringed. If multiple works are involved, provide a representative list.

### 2. Identification of Infringing Material
Specific identification of the material you claim is infringing, including:
- URL or specific location of the allegedly infringing content
- Description sufficient to locate the material

### 3. Your Contact Information
- Full legal name
- Mailing address
- Telephone number
- Email address

### 4. Good Faith Statement
A statement that you have a good faith belief that the use of the material is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.

### 5. Accuracy and Authority Statement
A statement, made under penalty of perjury, that:
- The information in your notice is accurate
- You are the copyright owner or authorized to act on the copyright owner's behalf

### 6. Signature
Your physical or electronic signature.

## 5. Response Timeline

Upon receiving a valid DMCA notice, we will:

- **Initial Response:** Acknowledge receipt within 48 hours
- **Investigation:** Review the claim and investigate the allegedly infringing content
- **Action Taken:** If valid, remove or disable access to the infringing material within 5-7 business days
- **User Notification:** Notify the user who posted the content (if applicable)

---

## 6. Counter-Notification Process

If you believe your content was removed in error or that you have authorization to use the material, you may file a counter-notification.

### Requirements for Counter-Notice

Your counter-notification must include:

1. **Identification:** Specific identification of the material that was removed
2. **Location:** Where the material appeared before it was removed
3. **Good Faith Statement:** Statement under penalty of perjury that you have a good faith belief the material was removed by mistake or misidentification
4. **Consent to Jurisdiction:** Statement that you consent to federal district court jurisdiction (or local jurisdiction if outside the US)
5. **Service of Process:** Statement that you will accept service of process from the person who filed the original DMCA notice
6. **Contact Information:** Your name, address, phone number, and email
7. **Signature:** Physical or electronic signature

### Counter-Notice Contact
- **Email:** support@sprunki.com
- **Subject Line:** "DMCA Counter-Notification"

## 8. False Claims

### Consequences of False DMCA Claims
- **Legal Liability:** Filing false DMCA notices may result in legal consequences
- **Damages:** You may be liable for damages, including costs and attorney fees
- **Perjury:** Knowingly making false statements in a DMCA notice constitutes perjury

### Good Faith Requirement
Only file DMCA notices if you genuinely believe your copyright has been infringed. Consider:
- Fair use and transformative work exceptions
- Whether you actually own the copyright
- Whether the use is authorized by law

## 9. Fair Use and Transformative Works

Sprunki Rejoyed is a fan-made modification that may constitute fair use or transformative work under copyright law. Factors considered include:

- **Purpose:** Non-commercial, educational, and entertainment purposes
- **Nature:** Transformative creative work
- **Amount:** Uses portions of original work in transformative manner
- **Market Effect:** Does not compete with or substitute for original work

We respect the intellectual property of the original Sprunki creators while creating original transformative content.
